Platform Basics

earn.cool is a decentralized micro-task platform running on the Solana network that connects social media content creators/projects with Web3 users. Advertisers (Creators) open tasks by locking budgets to increase social engagement. Users (Workers) connect their wallets and complete these tasks to instantly earn $EARN tokens.

The process is straightforward: An advertiser creates a campaign and locks the budget. A worker connects their wallet and X account to view active tasks in the Task Market. They open a task and complete the follow/like/repost action. When they return and verify, the system checks the interaction via the X API. Once approved, the earned share from the locked budget is instantly transferred to the worker's wallet.

Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the navbar and choose one of the supported Solana wallets (Phantom, Solflare). earn.cool is fully non-custodial and can never access your seed phrases or execute any transaction without your approval. It is extremely secure.

To start, you only need two things: a compatible Solana wallet (Phantom/Solflare) and an active X (Twitter) account. Once you connect both to the system, you can immediately start performing tasks and earning tokens.

Completing Work & Earning

Generally, you can complete X (Twitter) social tasks (following accounts, liking tweets, reposting). We also offer feedback campaigns where you can visit websites, review interfaces, or participate in creative design challenges.

Task visibility may be filtered based on your X account age, follower count, platform trust rank, and specific geographic/demographic criteria set by the advertiser. As you build trust, you will gain access to more tasks.

Social tasks are verified instantly, and payments reflect in your wallet within seconds. However, for custom feedback and meme design tasks that require manual review, there is a 12 to 24-hour review window. Rewards are released as soon as the advertiser approves.

Each task has a specific entry capacity. If the task reaches maximum capacity or the advertiser's escrow budget is depleted at the moment you click verify, the verification may fail or you may be placed in a waiting pool. In such cases, locked commissions are transferred to the Vault.

Ranks are trust signals calculated based on the number of successful verifications you complete on the platform and your anti-spam quality. Higher-ranked users gain access to exclusive higher-reward tasks and earn additional multipliers from Vault distributions.

Creating & Managing Jobs

Open the "Create Task" tab, choose one of the ready templates or specify the interaction type. After entering the target URL and reward amount, make the payment with your wallet. Your task will be verified and go live within seconds.

The standard platform service commission is 2%. However, if you want to filter the audience quality for engagement, additional costs apply: Verified account targeting adds +20% to the budget, and Sorsa Score > 0 targeting adds +15% additional commission. All these accumulated fees are collected in the Vault pool.
